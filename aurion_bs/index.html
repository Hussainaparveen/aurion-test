Our projects

Noida

Noida Metro

AuroTransit has implemented an NCMC RuPay CSC & QR-based AFC system and has provided all Station Level Equipment, Backoffice, and Mobile Application for the Noida Metro Rail Corporation (NMRC) in Noida, India. The consortium of State Bank of India, Aurionpro, and AuroTransit was awarded the project in 2017 and it is a contract for 10 years. The project is live since January 2019.
